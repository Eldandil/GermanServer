How to share

Sharing is easy. Send an email to clipcultexperiencies@gmail.com requesting an account bt

providing your name and email adress. You'll receive a confirmation with a login name and a password.

Record your video, publish it in youtube and send us the video id using the form at the end of this page.

The video id is at the end of your link. It is a sequence of characters.

For example, in the link https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M7lc1UVf-VE the video id is M7lc1UVf-VE
